ORIGINS

Unverified

The Birth

Date, location, and circumstances of birth remain disputed. Three countries claim him. All three are wrong. He has never corrected the record.

Unknown — Somewhere

Hospital records from Zurich, Naples, and Buenos Aires each carry a matching entry. Interpol investigators concluded the files were planted. They were right. That's all they got right.

Legend

First Known Language

By age 9, he was fluent in four languages. By age 11, he had invented a fifth — used exclusively in correspondence with a single unknown recipient.

circa 1995

Linguists at Oxford were shown a sample in 2018. Two of them resigned shortly after. The third converted to a hermetic lifestyle in rural Portugal. The language remains undeciphered.

2000s

Classified

The Prague Incident

A seventeen-year-old walks into the most secure private auction in Central Europe. Walks out with something that wasn't for sale. Nobody remembers his face.

2002 — Prague

Czech intelligence filed a report. The report was later acquired and shredded by a party using credentials belonging to a deceased diplomat. The auction house was sold six months later at a significant loss. Coincidence.

Complete

First Million

Made his first million at age 19. No documented source. No taxes filed. The IRS opened an inquiry and then quietly closed it eighteen months later. Nobody got a memo.

2004 — Unknown

His accountant from that period now lives on a private island and no longer practices. She has consistently refused interviews. She does, however, smile when his name is mentioned.

Legend

The Monaco Poker Game

A 28-hour poker game in a Monaco penthouse. Four heads of state, two oil oligarchs, and Brian Bors. He left with the deed to an island and a handshake agreement that still governs a small nation's trade policy today.

2007 — Monaco

Two players claimed to have been cheated. Both later withdrew the allegation simultaneously. Neither would say why. The island remains unregistered on any maritime chart.

2010s

Classified

Dark Sky Protocol

Vanished for eleven months. No sightings. No transactions. No communications. When he resurfaced, three governments had changed hands. He ordered a steak and said nothing about it.

2012–2013 — Classified

His phone was found in a dead drop in Marrakech. It contained a single photo: a chessboard mid-game. The position has never been identified as any known match.

Complete

The Buffett Summit

First meeting with Warren Buffett in Omaha. Official cover: a Cherry Coke taste test. Actual agenda: unknown. The meeting lasted nine hours. Berkshire's quarterly filing that season looked different from anything before.

2017 — Omaha, Nebraska

Three analysts tried to reverse-engineer the trades that followed. Two left the industry. The third now teaches philosophy at a community college in Vermont and seems much happier.

Complete

Roc Nation Summit — Monaco

Met Jay-Z over dinner in Monaco. What was said has never been repeated. What followed: 14 unexplained private jet routes, a joint venture nobody could locate in any filing, and mutual front-row appearances at industry events.

2019 — Monaco

A journalist who claimed to have a photo of the two men together later found that every photo on their camera from that night had been replaced with an identical image of an empty table set for two.

2020s

Complete

Operation Ghost Meridian

Six border crossings. Three passports. Zero traceable transactions. He delivered something no one will confirm exists to a buyer no one will name. Called it a "Tuesday."

2020 — Six Countries

Customs records across all six countries contain a matching three-hour gap. Officials investigated independently and reached the same conclusion: the gap was intentional, professional, and permanent.

Legend

The Riviera Accord

Brokered peace between two cartels over a single dinner. The restaurant never reopened — not because anything went wrong, but because the vibe afterward was simply unmatched and nothing could ever live up to it.

2021 — French Riviera

The chef who cooked the dinner has since moved to a mountain village and refuses to discuss the menu. He will say only that it was "the best and last thing" he ever made. He seems at peace.

Classified

The Vatican Recovery

Three priceless artifacts. Recovered from a sovereign nation's territory. No troops, no diplomacy, no headlines. The Pope wrote a letter in Latin. Bors had it framed. He placed it in storage.

2021 — Vatican City

Vatican records for this period contain a page designated "Per Voluntatem Dei" — By the Will of God. That page is blank. Archivists believe it is intentionally blank. They are correct.

Complete

The Louvre After Hours

A painting was returned. No questions were asked. In exchange, Bors received after-hours access on the first Sunday of every month. The cameras go dark. The guards look away. He stands alone in the wing for exactly one hour.

2022 — Paris

Security logs from those Sunday visits show only static. Staff on those nights are rotated from a separate roster. None of them have met each other. None of them ask why.

NOW

Ongoing

The Unfinished Game

Started in Tokyo. Involves a chess set. Spans at least four countries and an unknown number of players. It is not over. He finds this invigorating.

2024–Present — Unknown

Each move in the game corresponds to a real-world event that only becomes apparent six to eight months after the fact. Analysts who mapped the first seven moves have gone very quiet. The board currently shows a middle-game position analysts describe as "impossible to predict and terrifying to behold."
